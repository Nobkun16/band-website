Blackpink

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, debuting on August 8, 2016. Comprising Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa, they are known for their energetic performances and blend of K-pop, pop, hip-hop, and EDM. With hits like "DDU-DU DDU-DU" and "Kill This Love," BLACKPINK has gained international acclaim, becoming one of the most popular K-pop acts globally. They’ve collaborated with global artists like Dua Lipa and Selena Gomez and have achieved numerous awards, cementing their impact on global pop culture.

Member
Lisa (Lalisa Manoban)
Lisa is known for her incredible dance skills and charismatic rap performances. She is the only non-Korean member of BLACKPINK, hailing from Thailand, and she brings a unique flavor to the group. Lisa is often praised for her sharp and precise choreography, and she has a playful, outgoing personality that contrasts with her fierce stage persona. She has also made a mark as a fashion icon and is highly popular on social media.
Jennie (Kim Jennie)
Jennie is known for her chic and charismatic aura. She is a versatile performer, capable of delivering strong rap verses as well as melodic vocals. Jennie was the first member to release a solo single, titled "SOLO," which was a huge success. She has a fashion-forward image and is often seen as the trendsetter of the group, frequently collaborating with luxury fashion brands.
Rosé (Park Chaeyoung)
Rosé is recognized for her distinctive, soulful voice, which is a key element of BLACKPINK's sound. She is also an accomplished guitarist and pianist, often showcasing her musical talents in live performances. Rosé has a delicate yet powerful stage presence, and her unique vocal tone has made her stand out both within the group and in her solo work. She is fluent in English and has a warm, endearing personality.
Jisoo (Kim Jisoo)
Jisoo is known for her elegant beauty and calm demeanor. She has a rich, soothing voice that adds depth to BLACKPINK's music. Jisoo is also recognized for her strong stage presence and is often considered the group's visual, representing the face of BLACKPINK. Beyond singing, she has also ventured into acting and has a playful, warm personality that endears her to fans.

About

BLACKPINK was formed by YG Entertainment and debuted on August 8, 2016, with members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. After years of rigorous training, they quickly made a mark with their debut single album "Square One," featuring hits like "BOOMBAYAH" and "Whistle." BLACKPINK gained international fame with their unique blend of K-pop, pop, and hip-hop, and broke records with releases like "DDU-DU DDU-DU" and their album "The Album." Known for their powerful performances and trendsetting style, BLACKPINK has become one of the most influential K-pop groups globally, performing at major events like Coachella and collaborating with top international artists.
